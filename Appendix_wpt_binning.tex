\section{Comparison of \ptw results for the 6, 7 and 9 GeV binnings}
\label{sec:wpt_UnfBinning}

The \ptw unfolding has been studied in three different binnings of the $p^T$ truth spectrum: 6 GeV, 7 GeV and 9 GeV. 6 GeV binning is chosen to be the baseline for 5 TeV data, while 7 GeV is the baseline for 13 TeV. The comparisons of unfolding results, total uncertainties and unfolded \ptw of different predictions, are presented in the figures below in this section.
